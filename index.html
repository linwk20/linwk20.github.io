Wei-Kai (Allan) Lin 林惟凱

I am a Computer Science Ph.D. student at University of Rochester, advised by Yuhao Zhu. Previously, I earned B.E. in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University and M.Sc. in Intelligent Science from Peking University.
Research. My research spans across algorithm and hardware. I'm interested in AR/VR Graphics, Hardware Architecture, Imaging System, and AI (e.g. Embodied Intelligence, Generative AI). More details on my research page.
