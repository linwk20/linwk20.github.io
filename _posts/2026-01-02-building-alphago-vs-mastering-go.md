A question I can't stop thinking about lately: **Should we continue to focus on mastering Go as human players, or step back and build AlphaGo, so that humans no longer need to play Go at all?** Are there any tasks that AI cannot replace humans in?

To answer this question, I will attempt to replace as much of my daily work as possible with AI and explore the current boundaries. Although AI will undoubtedly become stronger in the future, I want to probe the current limits now, so that I don't continue to cultivate skills that may soon become useless.
